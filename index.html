💰 Module 6 — How to Talk About Money
Pricing Calculator

Step 1 — Your Numbers
💡 Pricing Principles
Price from value, not time
What is the transformation worth to your client? A $10k result justifies a $2k price.
Stop apologising for your price
State your price clearly. Pause. Let them respond. The discomfort you feel is normal.
Explain your value, not your hours
"This includes X, Y, and Z, which helps you achieve [outcome]." Not: "I spend about 4 hours per week."
Handle hesitation with confidence
"I understand it's an investment. What's holding you back?" Then listen.

Module 4 — Where Should You Show Up?
Choose ONE primary platform
Where do your ideal clients spend time? Go there. Master it before adding another.
The 3-type content formula
Educate: Teach something valuable (40%)
Connect: Share your story (40%)
Offer: Tell people how to work with you (20%)
Consistency beats volume
3 posts per week for 12 months beats 30 posts in January and burning out.

Why you need an email list
Social media accounts can disappear. Your email list is an asset you own.
Create something simple to offer
A checklist, guide, or resource list — something genuinely useful your ideal client would trade their email for.
What to send
One email per week is enough. Share something valuable. Always end with one clear call to action.
